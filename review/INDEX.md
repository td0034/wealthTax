## Reading order for someone new to the project

For policy people: `11`, then `12`, then read the `wealth_tax/`
paper itself.

For methodology people: `08`, `09`, then the `cap_v2/` paper, then
`11` for the punchline.

For round-2 reviewers of the cap proposal: `08` (the critique we
addressed), `09` (the empirical response), `10` (the conceptual
response).
